PART 1 — THE THREE REGIMES & A2 SUB-ZONES
REGIMEW-RSIMARKET STATEYOUR JOB
A   HOPE ≥ 50 Institutional accumulation — bull trend intact Deploy on dips. Hold. Do not sell without a trigger.
B   WATCH 40 – 49 No-Man's Land — whipsaw risk high No new buys. Trim excess if from A. Wait for recovery.
C   FEAR < 40 Institutional distribution — bear trend confirmed 100% cash. No exceptions. No averaging down.
W-RSI = Weekly RSI (Relative Strength Index sampled on weekly closes)  ·  WMA20 = 20-period Weighted Moving Average of NAV (linearly weighted, not weekly)  ·  Regime Watch threshold: Weekly RSI 40–49 means below 50 (Hope) and at or above 40 — the exact code boundary is wrsi < 50 && wrsi >= 40
Regime Hope Sub-Zones   (shown by the Regime Hope scanners)
SUB-ZONECONDITIONMEANING
INVEST D-RSI < 40 or Delta < −15 or PC Alert & Delta ≤ 15 Active entry signal present. Deploy now.
HOLD Regime Hope (W-RSI ≥ 50) — no entry or exit signal active D-RSI 40–50, no PC, Delta −15 to +15. Stay invested.
PROFIT Delta > +15 (slingshot extended) Trim 10–25% to lock partial gain. Do not fully exit.
Delta = D-RSI minus W-RSI. Slingshot stretched beyond +15 signals mean-reversion risk. PC Alert alone does NOT push a fund into PROFIT zone — only Delta > 15 does.
PART 2 — BUY SOP
Step 1 — Gate Check   (ALL must pass before any buy)
□W-RSI ≥ 50  (Regime Hope confirmed — scanner shows "INVEST" or "HOLD")
□TSL floor intact — NAV is above the LOWESS adaptive floor (no red TSL badge)
□No active NC (Negative Consolidation) breakdown signal
□Available cash > ₹500. Concentration Guard not red (<80% deployed).
✗If ANY gate fails → DO NOT BUY. The Pre-Trade Checklist (F3/F9) enforces this automatically.
Step 2 — Signal Confirmation   (at least one required)
SIGNALMEANING & REGIME STATESIZE
D-RSI < 35 Regime Hope (W-RSI ≥ 50) · Daily RSI deeply oversold below 35 — extreme dip inside a structural bull. Highest conviction entry. App alert: 🟢 OVERSOLD RSI<40 ➔ BUY TRANCHE BUY 50%
D-RSI < 40 Regime Hope (W-RSI ≥ 50) · Daily RSI oversold below 40 — standard pullback inside the bull trend. App alert: 🟢 OVERSOLD RSI<40 ➔ BUY TRANCHE BUY 25%
PC Breakout Regime Hope (W-RSI ≥ 50) · Price breaks above a tight 5-bar consolidation box (≤2% range) sitting near WMA20 (within 4%). Compressed energy release upward. App alert: 🟢 PC BREAKOUT ➔ BUY TRANCHE BUY 25%
Slingshot   Δ < −15 Regime Hope (W-RSI ≥ 50) · Daily RSI is lagging Weekly RSI by more than 15 points (Delta = D-RSI − W-RSI < −15) — coiled spring, mean-reversion setup. App alert: 🟢 SLINGSHOT Δ<−15 ➔ BUY TRANCHE BUY 10–25%
Regime Watch→Hope Breakout Regime Watch→Hope (W-RSI crosses above 50) · Weekly RSI was 40–49 (Watch) and has just moved to ≥ 50 (Hope). Fresh structural upgrade — wait 1–2 days for confirmation before buying. App alert: 🟢 REGIME WATCH BREAKOUT ➔ BUY ZONE BUY 25%
ChoCh Breakout Regime Hope (W-RSI ≥ 50) · Price breaks above the most recent confirmed swing high — Change-of-Character signal confirming structural bullish intent. BUY 10%
Step 3 — Position Sizing Rules
●Never deploy more than 50% of total portfolio in a single tranche.
●Maximum 3 open tranches at any time. Track them via the Tranche Tracker (F8).
●Check the Deployment Meter (Gap 6) — stay under 80% invested in Regime Hope.
⚠️If portfolio > 80% concentrated → BUY 50% is auto-disabled (Concentration Guard, Gap 3).
Step 4 — Log the Rationale   (enter 3 words before every trade in F3 checklist)
✎"Regime Hope dip."  |  "PC breakout signal."  |  "Slingshot Δ confirmed."  |  "ChoCh breakout."
PART 3 — SELL SOP
Mandatory Exits   — Non-Negotiable, Same-Day Execution
🚨TSL Breach — Any Regime: NAV drops below LOWESS adaptive floor → Sell ALL tranches immediately. Regime and RSI level are irrelevant — the structural floor is broken. App alert: 🚨 TSL BREACHED ➔ EXECUTE TSL SELL
🚨Regime Fear — Weekly RSI drops below 40: Institutional distribution confirmed → Sell ALL. Move to 100% cash. No exceptions, no averaging down. App alert: 🚨 REGIME CRASH ➔ SELL ALL
⚠️WMA20 Breach — Regime Hope (Weekly RSI ≥ 50): NAV drops below 20-period Weighted Moving Average while Regime is still Hope → Sell 20% as discipline trim. Not a full exit unless regime also breaks below 50. App alert: ⚠️ WMA BREACHED ➔ REMIND SELL 20%
⚠️NC (Negative Consolidation) Breakdown — Any Regime: Price breaks below tight consolidation box low → Tighten TSL to box low. Cancel any pending buy orders. App alert: 🚨 NC BREAKDOWN ➔ DO NOT BUY
Profit-Taking Triggers   — Discretionary but Scored in Session Audit
CONDITION & REGIME STATEACTION

Regime Hope (W-RSI ≥ 50) · D-RSI > 70 — Daily RSI overbought while regime intact. Trim, do not fully exit. Sell 10% — trim the winner, not full exit

Never Sell Because:
✗Market "looks expensive" while W-RSI is still in Regime Hope. Indicators, not opinions.
✗News scared you  (Regime Hope absorbs bad news into the uptrend; that's its job).
✗You already made 15%  (let winners run — the What-If panel F6 shows the cost of early exits).

PART 5 — MOST PROFITABLE PROCESS: THE A-B-C ROTATION
1
Universe Scan — Sunday
Weekly · takes 60 seconds
Run RRG Leading + RRG Improving scanner (home screen, green/blue buttons). Identify the top 3 funds with highest RS vs Nifty 50 (RS = fund NAV ÷ Nifty 50 NAV) and positive RS-Momentum (RS above HMA(30)) across all 58 instruments. Then run Regime Hope → Invest scanner to cross-check which of those 3 have an active D-RSI or Slingshot entry signal today.
2
Asset Allocator — Sunday
Weekly · confirms your top 2 picks
Run Asset Allocator for your AMC (home screen → 📊 Asset Allocator). It scores all funds on BAF gate + conviction and outputs a weighted 60:40 or 50:50 allocation. Trust the output — do not override it with gut feel. Activate the Trigger Monitor inside the allocator to get live 3-condition daily alerts.
3
Deploy on First Dip
As signalled — use Live Cockpit to confirm
Wait for D-RSI < 40 in the top-ranked fund after confirming Regime Hope (Weekly RSI ≥ 50). That is your entry — not before the dip arrives. Open Live Cockpit to see the current RSI, WMA20, and TSL in real time. Enter the rationale in the F3 Pre-Trade Checklist before executing.
4
Monitor via Trigger Monitor (3 Conditions)
Daily — 2-minute check inside Asset Allocator
C1 — Regime Fear: Either holding drops to Regime Fear (W-RSI < 40) → Exit immediately.
C2 — Momentum Retreat: Holding falls below BAF fund + 2% momentum → Retreat 100% to BAF as defensive hold.
C3 — Momentum Swap: An unowned Regime Hope fund outperforms your rank-2 holding by >3% for 2+ consecutive weeks → Swap into the stronger fund.
5
Rotate, Don't Panic-Exit
On trigger fire — execute within 1 trading day
When C1/C2/C3 fires you are not leaving the market — you are rotating into a stronger fund or retreating to BAF as a defensive hold. Compounding stays alive throughout. Use BAF ↔ NPS Switch Planner to track your 4 free annual NPS scheme switches when executing rotations.
